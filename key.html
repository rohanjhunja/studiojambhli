bapu key messages
Bapu Trust
Bapu Trust for Research on Mind and Discourse has created a set of multi lingual books on key concepts for the use of mental health practitioners.
These are core concepts used by field professionals, in easy to understand language.
The design helps in making the material easy to read and understand fast. It is developed as a book to be carried on the field while training, keeping in mind production costs and weight.
